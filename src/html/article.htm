JAME LEAVES VIRTOUS.PRO

  • GAMING
  • VIRTUSPRO
  • ESPORT
03.03.2025

Jami "Jame" Ali, former captainof the squad Virtus.pro In CS2, the champion and MVP of the IEM Rio 2022 major, who played 856 cards for the Bears, is leaving our club.

Nikolay Petrosyan, CEO Virtus.pro saying:

Saying goodbye to players, especially such significant and legendary ones as Zheka, is always difficult. Nevertheless, we made this decision based on the best interests of Jami himself. His passion for the game is still strong, he wants to keep playing and we went to meet.
